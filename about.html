Who are we?

Business Data Automation is the Master Reseller of world famous Accounting, Inventory and Payroll Management Software "QuickBooks" for whole Bangladesh. This organization has been established in 2011 with the vision is to implement the modern system of accounting procedure in Bangladesh.

History of Company

Business Data Automation Bangladesh is established in July-2011 with a specific mission and vision. This organization works only in accounting and finance sector to make digitalized any company or corporate house in its territory. In Bangladesh, there is huge scope to work in Accounting information system. From this idea, founders of this organization had decided to work in this field by forming this organization investing their Talents and experiences.

Our Main Task

We are mainly involved in sale of "QuickBooks"software as well as provide necessary training on it. We are also committed to increase the capability of Human Resources in Accounting profession in Bangladesh.

Our Mission

Our mission is to implement the modern accounting Information system in Bangladesh by automating the numerical data management system of business organizations.
